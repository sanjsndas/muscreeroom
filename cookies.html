1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are tiny text files that are stored on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently, as well as to provide reporting information and to remember your preferences for future visits. Imagine them as tiny digital markers left by our website on your device, helping us recognize you when you return. These files contain small amounts of information, such as a unique identifier, and are sent back to the originating website on each subsequent visit, or to another website that recognizes that cookie.

Cookies can be "persistent" or "session" cookies. Persistent cookies remain on your personal computer or mobile device when you go offline, while session cookies are deleted as soon as you close your web browser. They serve various functions, from enabling basic site navigation and security to personalizing your content and delivering targeted advertisements. Without certain cookies, many website functionalities would simply not work, or would be significantly less convenient. They are a fundamental part of modern web browsing, designed to make your online experience smoother and more relevant.

Beyond traditional cookies, other similar technologies exist, such as web beacons, pixel tags, and local storage. These technologies also help us collect information about your interactions with our site, track user behavior, and provide personalized content. This policy covers our use of all such technologies that function similarly to cookies. Our commitment is to transparency, ensuring you understand how these digital tools contribute to your MushCreeRoom experience.

2. How We Use Cookies

MushCreeRoom uses cookies for several purposes, aiming to enhance your experience and provide relevant services. Our use of cookies falls into several categories, each serving a distinct function to ensure our digital forest thrives and offers you the best possible journey. We carefully select and manage the cookies we deploy, always with your privacy and user experience in mind.

  • Strictly Necessary Cookies: These cookies are essential for the operation of our Site. They enable you to navigate around the website and use its features, such as accessing secure areas, adding items to your shopping cart, and making purchases. Without these cookies, services like online shopping or account login cannot be provided. They are automatically placed on your device when you visit our site, as they are fundamental to its functionality.
  • Performance and Analytics Cookies: These cookies collect information about how you use our Site, such as which pages you visit most often, and if you encounter any error messages. This data helps us understand and analyze user behavior, allowing us to measure and improve the performance of our Site. For example, we might use them to identify popular "CreeRoom Journal" articles or to optimize the "Shop Kits" layout. All information these cookies collect is aggregated and therefore anonymous. It is only used to improve how our website works.
  • Functionality Cookies: These cookies allow our Site to remember choices you make (such as your username, language, or the region you are in) and provide enhanced, more personal features. For instance, they can remember your "MushMood Filter" preferences or save items in your wishlist. They can also be used to remember changes you have made to text size, fonts, and other parts of web pages that you can customize. The information these cookies collect may be anonymized and they cannot track your browsing activity on other websites.
  • Advertising and Targeting Cookies: These cookies are used to deliver advertisements more relevant to you and your interests. They are also used to limit the number of times you see an advertisement and help measure the effectiveness of the advertising campaigns. They are usually placed by advertising networks with our permission. They remember that you have visited a website and this information is shared with other organizations, such as advertisers. For example, after visiting our "Wellness Kit" section, you might see MushCreeRoom ads on other sites.
  • Social Media Cookies: These cookies are set by a range of social media services that we have added to the site to enable you to share our content with your friends and networks. They are capable of tracking your browser across other sites and building up a profile of your interests. This may impact the content and messages you see on other websites you visit. If you do not allow these cookies, you may not be able to use or see these sharing tools.

By understanding these categories, you gain a clearer picture of how cookies contribute to your overall MushCreeRoom experience. We are committed to using cookies responsibly, ensuring they serve to enhance your journey rather than compromise your privacy.

4. Your Choices Regarding Cookies

You have the power to manage your cookie preferences and control how your data is collected and used. While strictly necessary cookies are essential for the Site's basic functionality, you can choose to accept or decline other types of cookies. Your choices can significantly impact your experience on our Site.

  • Browser Settings: Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can typically set your browser to notify you when you receive a cookie, giving you the chance to decide whether to accept it. You can also configure your browser to block all cookies or to delete specific cookies that have already been placed. Be aware that blocking all cookies may impact the functionality of many websites, including ours.
  • Opt-Out Links: Many advertising networks offer mechanisms to opt out of targeted advertising. For example, you can opt out of Google's use of cookies by visiting Google's Ads Settings. You can also visit the Network Advertising Initiative Opt-Out Page or the Digital Advertising Alliance Opt-Out Page to learn more about opting out of interest-based advertising.
  • Cookie Consent Banner: Upon your first visit to MushCreeRoom.com, you will see a cookie consent banner at the bottom of the page. This banner provides you with options to accept all cookies, decline non-essential cookies, or manage your preferences directly. You can revisit your preferences at any time by clicking on a link (if provided) or clearing your browser's cookies.
  • Specific Tool Settings: Some third-party tools, like the "GrowMeter App Integration," may have their own in-app settings for data collection and preferences. We encourage you to explore these settings within the respective applications.

Please note that if you delete cookies or refuse to accept them, you might not be able to use all of the features we offer, you may not be able to store your preferences, and some of our pages might not display properly. Your ability to engage with personalized features like the "MushMood Filter" might also be affected. We respect your choices and aim to provide you with the necessary tools to manage your privacy effectively.
